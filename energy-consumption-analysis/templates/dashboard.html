WattWise — Energy Consumption Intelligence Dashboard

"Analyzing electricity usage patterns to identify trends, peak demand periods, and abnormal consumption spikes."

Project Objective

This dashboard analyzes electricity consumption data to uncover patterns in energy demand. By visualizing consumption trends and detecting anomalies, the system helps energy providers better understand electricity usage behavior and identify critical demand periods.

Section 1 — Electricity Consumption Over Time

This section visualizes electricity consumption over time to observe overall demand patterns and long-term trends.

Analytical Insight

  • The data illustrates whether consumption follows an increasing or decreasing trajectory across observed cycles.
  • Notable spikes in usage are clearly mapped out against standard baselines as days advance.
  • The overall demand trend maintains a steady growth correlating to long-term consumption factors.

Section 2 — State or Region Wise Consumption

This section compares electricity consumption across different states or regions.

Analytical Insight

  • Maharashtra registers as the state with the highest consistent power consumption per average cycle.
  • Conversely, Karnataka maintains the lowest baseline consumption volume when averaged.
  • These variations are driven directly by differences in structural industries, climate zones, and relative population clusters in those locations.

Section 3 — Monthly Energy Consumption Trend

This section aggregates electricity consumption by month to identify seasonal demand patterns.

Analytical Insight

  • Monthly demand shifts indicate increasing load requirements transitioning towards warmer periods.
  • Possible seasonal factors deeply affecting consumption range from industrial ramping phases to standard cooling appliance overheads as local climates warm up.

Section 4 — Peak Load Period Analysis

This section identifies the time periods when electricity demand reaches its highest levels.

Analytical Insight

  • A critical peak day captures the highest concurrent energy usage explicitly mapping the grid's maximum capacity stress.
  • Tracking these occurrences holds vital importance for grid management to prevent systemic blackouts and dynamically align baseline supply production effectively.

Section 5 — Anomaly Detection (Abnormal Spikes)

This section highlights abnormal spikes in electricity consumption using anomaly detection techniques.

Analytical Insight

  • Scattered anomaly flags pinpoint precisely where abnormal high consumption patterns occur visually.
  • Possible causes might include abrupt weather changes causing unpredicted demand, faulty technical machinery, or heavy unscheduled industrial operations draining local capacity.

Final Insights

  • Electricity consumption shows a steady trend over time.

Conclusion
